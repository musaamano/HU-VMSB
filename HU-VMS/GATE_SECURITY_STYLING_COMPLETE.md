## Styling Details

### Color Scheme (Matching Fuel Station)
- **Primary Blue**: #4a90e2
- **Secondary Blue**: #357abd
- **Light Blue**: #e7f3ff to #cfe7ff
- **Background**: #f5f7fa to #c3cfe2 gradient
- **Text Dark**: #1f2937
- **Text Medium**: #374151
- **Text Light**: #6b7280

### Status Colors
- **Approved/Success**: #10b981 (green)
- **Pending/Warning**: #f59e0b (orange)
- **Rejected/Error**: #ef4444 (red)
- **Info**: #4a90e2 (blue)

### Shadows & Borders
- **Multi-layered shadows**: 3-5 layers for depth
- **Gradient borders**: Using background-clip technique
- **Inset highlights**: For 3D appearance
- **Blue-tinted shadows**: Matching theme
- **Border radius**: 16-24px for modern look

### Animations
1. **fadeInUp**: Content entrance (0.5s)
2. **scaleIn**: Card entrance (0.5s)
3. **pulse**: Live indicator (2s infinite)
4. **Hover transforms**: Elevation and scale
5. **Staggered delays**: Cards appear sequentially
