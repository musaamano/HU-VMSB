## Visual Effects Summary

### Shadow Layers
- Primary: Large spread for depth (0 10px 30px)
- Secondary: Medium spread for definition (0 6px 16px)
- Tertiary: Small spread for subtle lift (0 3px 8px)
- Inset: Top highlight (inset 0 1px 0)
- Glow: Color-specific outer glow on hover
